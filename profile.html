Our story began when a deadly pathogen hit the world on March 11, 2020;

and closed millions of schools in the next few weeks. All children, of all ages, and in all countries, were affected; and their education was shut down to protect them from potential harm and even death. It was then when Tagpros Children was founded by US educator Chris Manansala as he wrote an online pedagogy in his home in Virginia, to be helmed by outstanding, english speaking, and passionate teachers to supplement education of children online. Later, he brought in award-winning educator Jim Tuscano from the Philippines; and his long-time friend Jo Garde, a top marketing executive in Asia, to help him further develop a Professional Learning Community (PLC) he named Tagpros Children.

FOLKLORE
Tagpros is an acronym for Talented and Gifted Professionals, a spin-off from Tagalogpro, a teacher exchange initiative Chris is trying to establish at that time to help Filipino educators get visiting and training opportunities in the US school system. At the height of the pandemic, he took a detour to virtual education when he saw his own school-aged children, who are both doing well in school, were suddenly having a hard time learning because their teachers were struggling to convey lessons online.
SLOGAN

Mission Driven. Children Focused.

We care about one thing: children. And we’re working to meet their needs in all sorts of ways: educating their teachers, providing enrichment to their parents, collaborating with schools, and creating positive experiences that bring families and communities together.

OUR BELIEFS
While we believe in providing the best learning experience for kids, it’s also our belief that in order to achieve this, we need to help adults get equipped virtually so they can provide education and social emotional learning support for children. We’re huge in teachers’ preparation and parent enrichment, which is why we train thousands of teachers, parents, and even school for free through our CSR initiative; and inform them of different ways to help kids learn and thrive, both at home and online.
OUR PARTNERS

"It takes a village to raise a child," says a popular African proverb. Our global community, whom we call partners, are at the heart of children’s learning.

We empower a global community with streaming shows and webinars. We provide opportunities for teachers, parents, and various education stakeholders to interface and collaborate with others from around the world to promote the formation of a global PLC to support kids’ education and social emotional learning.

OUR LOGO

A teacher or a student pushing or reaching the globe (circle) forward. Thus, both of them signifies two different meanings: (i) teachers are the prime movers of the next generation; and (ii) students are the hope and future of the world they are trying to understand and nurture. The different colors symbolizes a young and vibrant society that respects diversity, equity, and inclusion.
